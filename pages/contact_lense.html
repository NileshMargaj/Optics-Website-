Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to your most common queries.

Can I order contact lenses online in India?

Yes, Specsmakers offers a wide range of contact lenses with quick delivery across India.

Are disposable lenses good for beginners?

Yes, daily disposable lenses are a great starting point. They're simple to use, require no cleaning, and are discarded after each wear, making them a safe and low-maintenance option for first-time users. Trial lenses are also available to help you get used to them before committing.

What types of lenses do you have?

Monthly disposable contact lenses -spherical ,Toric (For astigmatism) ,and color contact lenses -spherical and zero power.

How do I care for my monthly lenses?

Clean your lenses daily with solution. Clean the lens case every 3 days, and replace the case if it becomes dirty or damaged. Changing the case every 2 months is advisable.
